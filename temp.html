Ba Na Hills, Vietnam

Adventure by Krishna, Manju & Priyanka

In August 2025, Krishna, Manju, and Priyanka embarked on a spectacular journey to Ba Na Hills in Vietnam. Nestled in the mountains near Da Nang, this enchanting destination offered breathtaking views, thrilling cable cars, and the iconic Golden Bridge held by giant hands.

They enjoyed exploring the French village, tasting delicious Vietnamese street food, and wandering through colorful flower gardens. Every moment was filled with laughter, joy, and the magic of Vietnam's beauty.
